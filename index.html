All Commands

Use slash commands to make Groovie join and then use voice commands for the rest

Join - The Music Bot will join the voice channel
Play - Plays the mentioned track
Pause - Pauses the ongoing track
Resume - Resumes a paused track
Unpause - Unpauses the paused track
Skip - Skips the ongoing track
Queue - Displays the current queue of tracks
Shuffle - Shuffles the queued tracks
Remove - Remove desired track from the queue
Leave - The Music Bot leaves the voice channel
Stop - The Music Bot stops and leaves the voice channel
Disconnect - The Music Bot disconnect from the voice channel
Lyrics - Shows the user the lyrics for the current track
Current - Shows the progress of the current track
